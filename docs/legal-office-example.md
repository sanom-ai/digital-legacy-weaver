# Legal Office Example (Operational Companion Model)

This example shows how a legal office can use Digital Legacy Weaver as an operational companion without replacing legal will documents.

## Scope

Legal office handles:

1. legal will drafting and enforceability
2. executor identity verification
3. jurisdiction-specific legal procedure

Digital Legacy Weaver handles:

1. digital asset reference orchestration
2. secure release workflow controls
3. audit-ready event trail for digital execution steps

## Example workflow

### Step 1: Client onboarding

1. Client signs legal engagement and formal will process.
2. Client enables legal disclaimer and consent in app.
3. Client records executor and advisor contact references.

### Step 2: Digital inventory mapping

1. Client adds partner connectors (wallet, exchange, cloud, social).
2. Client stores encrypted references and operational notes.
3. Client selects release modes and thresholds.

### Step 3: Periodic review

1. Office schedules policy review every 6-12 months.
2. Client updates account/asset references as life changes.
3. Office validates that legal and digital references stay aligned.

### Step 4: Trigger and execution

1. If conditions are met, system starts controlled release workflow.
2. Office verifies legal prerequisites before executing external steps.
3. Office uses audit logs and references to complete digital handover.

### Step 5: Post-case closure

1. Office records case closure summary.
2. Access controls are paused or disabled.
3. Retention and cleanup policy is applied.

## Recommended office controls

1. Two-person review for final release confirmation.
2. Incident pause authority for on-call legal operations.
3. Monthly review of security events and stale heartbeat checks.
4. Use release checklist before any high-impact action.

## Client-facing language template

"This system does not replace your legal will.  
It helps your authorized representative understand your digital asset map and execute secure operational steps under legal guidance."
