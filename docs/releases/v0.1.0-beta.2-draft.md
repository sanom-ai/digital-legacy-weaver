# Digital Legacy Weaver v0.1.0-beta.2

Private-first beta focused on clearer privacy posture, simpler preset selection, and sharper product communication for the next closed-beta cohort.

## Release type

1. Closed-beta release for the next cohort.
2. Focused on private-first clarity, preset selection, and release communication.
3. Intended to improve usability without changing the product boundary.

## Highlights

1. Privacy preset selector is now available in onboarding and safety settings with plain-language options:
- `Confidential`
- `Minimal`
- `Audit-heavy`
2. Dashboard now shows the active privacy preset without requiring a trip into settings.
3. PTN privacy profile presets are ready for policy selection, smoke tests, and runtime alignment.
4. Release communication now consistently reinforces the product boundary: technical companion, not legal will.

## Why this matters

1. Closed-beta users can understand privacy trade-offs faster.
2. Operators can explain the product more consistently across release pages, screenshots, and onboarding.
3. PTN policy posture and app settings now feel like one coordinated system instead of separate layers.

## Private-first Profiles

1. `Confidential`: highest privacy posture; keeps only high-level outcome markers and avoids detailed requirement traces.
2. `Minimal`: recommended default for beta; keeps sanitized control-state only.
3. `Audit-heavy`: best for operational review; keeps sanitized evidence and owner references for deeper analysis.

Reference note:

1. `docs/releases/v0.1.0-beta-private-first-note.md`

## Important boundary reminder

1. Digital Legacy Weaver is a technical companion.
2. It helps coordinate secure delivery and recovery workflows.
3. It is not a legal will.
4. It is not a legal adjudication service.
5. Beneficiaries still verify legal entitlement directly with the destination provider and applicable legal process.

## Recommended release copy

1. Use `technical companion` as the core product phrase.
2. Avoid any wording that suggests legal authority, automatic inheritance validity, or guaranteed legal outcome.

## Suggested validation before publish

1. Run `quality` and `flutter-quality` on latest `main`.
2. Confirm release assets and preset selector wording in the app build.
3. Keep beta messaging consistent with "technical companion" across release page and screenshots.
