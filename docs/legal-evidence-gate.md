# Legal Verification Handoff (Technical Layer)

## Position

Digital Legacy Weaver does **not** collect or adjudicate legal evidence.  
The platform is a technical coordination layer that notifies and orchestrates release workflow only.

## What this means in practice

1. We notify according to owner-configured policy and recipient mapping.
2. We provide secure access workflow (one-time link + second factor).
3. We do not accept legal document uploads for entitlement decisions.
4. Beneficiaries must submit legal documents directly to destination apps/providers.

## Destination provider responsibility

Each destination app/provider must perform its own legal verification process, such as:

1. death certificate checks
2. family registry/heir validation
3. jurisdiction-specific compliance review

## Why this boundary is important

1. reduces legal-liability ambiguity for orchestration platform
2. preserves provider-side compliance authority
3. keeps the product role clear: technical layer, not legal authority
