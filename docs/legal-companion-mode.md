# Legal Companion Mode

Digital Legacy Weaver is positioned as a legal companion, not a legal replacement.
It is a technical layer for secure orchestration, policy enforcement, and auditable workflow controls.

## Positioning statement

1. The product supports secure coordination and controlled release of digital legacy guidance.
2. The product does not replace a legally valid will process in every jurisdiction.
3. Users should align digital instructions with legal counsel and formal estate planning.
4. The product does not collect legal evidence to adjudicate beneficiary entitlement.
5. Beneficiaries must verify legal entitlement directly with destination apps/providers.

## UX and communication requirements

1. Show legal disclaimer at onboarding and before enabling legacy release.
2. Require explicit consent before automated release can be activated.
3. Keep audit logs available for executor/trust review when permitted.

## Recommended legal companion workflow

1. User configures digital release policy in app.
2. User maps guidance to beneficiary contacts and recovery pathways.
3. User stores legal reference metadata:
- will reference number
- executor contact
- legal advisor contact
4. User reviews settings periodically and confirms alive-check.

## Product language to use

Use:

1. "Digital legacy coordination"
2. "Secure release workflow"
3. "Legal companion"

Avoid:

1. "This app is your legal will"
2. "Legally binding by default"
3. "No lawyer needed"
