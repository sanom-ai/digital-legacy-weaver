# Positioning by Audience

Use this guide to present Digital Legacy Weaver consistently without over-claiming.

## Core message (all audiences)

Digital Legacy Weaver is a private-first technical coordination layer for digital legacy and self-recovery workflows.

## 1) Developer audience

Primary angle:

1. Policy-driven infrastructure for secure release orchestration.
2. Supabase + edge-function architecture with auditable controls.
3. Integration-ready flow for wallet/app/provider collaboration.

Use language like:

1. "policy-as-code"
2. "runtime safety controls"
3. "observable release workflow"
4. "technical-layer boundary by design"

Avoid:

1. "fully production-ready for any scale" (until stable criteria are met)
2. "legal automation engine"

## 2) Partner / operations audience

Primary angle:

1. Operational risk reduction (false-trigger gates, safety switches, audit logs).
2. Repeatable runbooks and CI reliability checks.
3. Clear handoff model to destination provider legal verification.

Use language like:

1. "controlled release flow"
2. "incident-ready operations"
3. "handoff to provider entitlement process"

Avoid:

1. "we verify legal entitlement for you"
2. "guaranteed legal compliance in all jurisdictions"

## 3) Investor / strategic audience

Primary angle:

1. Category: digital legacy safety infrastructure.
2. Product wedge: technical recovery + orchestration + partner handoff.
3. Expansion path: partner ecosystem integrations, compliance tooling, enterprise controls.

Use language like:

1. "high-trust digital continuity infrastructure"
2. "developer-first platform with partner network effects"
3. "responsible-by-design legal boundary"

Avoid:

1. vanity claims without usage evidence
2. legal replacement claims

## Recommended narrative stack

1. Problem: digital continuity and inheritance workflows are fragmented and high-risk.
2. Product: policy-based orchestration with secure unlock and auditable runtime controls.
3. Boundary: technical companion, not legal authority.
4. Proof: CI/runtime checks, release artifacts, runbooks, measurable operational metrics.
5. Roadmap: reliability maturity and ecosystem integrations.
